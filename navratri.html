HAPPY NAVRATRI 2022

The Navratri festival is considered one of the most promising in the Hindu faith. The nine-day event commemorates the worship of Maa Durga in her nine manifestations. Navratri is celebrated twice a year. Twice a year, once in the month of Chaitra and once in Sharad.
Wishing a very Happy Chaitra Navratri to you. May Maa Durga is always there to impart you with great strength to fight all your challenges
